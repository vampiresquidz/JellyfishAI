Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

HypnoFlow ("we", "us") builds a personalized AI hypnosis and meditation app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We keep it plain because your trust matters more than legalese.

The short version. We collect the minimum needed to run the app: your waitlist email, an account identifier if you sign in, and the sessions you create. Your meditations live on your device and in your own iCloud. We don't sell your data, and we never share the personal things you type with advertisers.

1. Information we collect

Waitlist

If you join the waitlist on our website, we collect the email address and username you enter, so we can tell you when the app launches. That's it.

Account (Sign in with Apple)

If you create an account, we use Sign in with Apple. Apple gives us a stable, anonymous identifier for you and — only if you choose to share it — your name and email. We use these to recognize you across devices and personalize the app. We never receive your Apple password.

Content you create

When you generate a session you tell us your goal and intention (for example, "help me stop waking at 3am"). We use this text to write and narrate your session. Your finished sessions are stored on your device and, if you're signed into iCloud, synced privately through Apple CloudKit to your other devices. We can't read your CloudKit library.

Purchases

Subscriptions and credit purchases are processed by Apple. We use RevenueCat to manage entitlements and receipts. We never see or store your payment card details.

Diagnostics

We may collect basic, non-identifying crash and performance data to keep the app stable. We do not build advertising profiles about you.

2. How we use your information

  • To generate and narrate your personalized sessions.
  • To save and sync your library across your devices.
  • To manage your subscription, credits, and account.
  • To let you know when the app launches (waitlist).
  • To fix bugs, prevent abuse, and improve the app.

3. Service providers we share with

We share limited data with the vendors that make the app work. They act on our behalf and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes:

  • Apple — Sign in with Apple, in-app purchases, and iCloud storage of your library.
  • RevenueCat — subscription and credit management.
  • ElevenLabs — text-to-speech. The text of your session script is sent to ElevenLabs so it can generate the narration audio.
  • Web3Forms — delivers your waitlist signup to us.
  • DigitalOcean — hosts our website.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data retention

Your sessions remain until you delete them or remove the app. Waitlist emails are kept until launch or until you ask us to remove you. Account data is kept while your account is active; you can sign out or ask us to delete it.

5. Your choices & rights

  • Access or delete — email us to request a copy or deletion of your data.
  • Your library — delete any session in the app, or remove the app to delete local data.
  • Sign out — sign out anytime in Settings.
  • Unsubscribe — manage or cancel your subscription in Settings (via Apple), and opt out of waitlist email using the link in any message.
  • Depending on where you live (e.g., EEA, UK, California), you may have additional rights to access, correct, or erase your data. Contact us to exercise them.

6. Children

HypnoFlow is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country), and we don't knowingly collect their data. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we'll remove it.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including Apple's Keychain for your account identifier and encrypted transport. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to keep your information safe.

8. International users

We and our providers may process your information in countries other than your own. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for these transfers.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We'll revise the "last updated" date above and, for material changes, provide a more prominent notice.

10. Contact us

Questions or requests about your privacy? Email support@hypnoflow.dev.
